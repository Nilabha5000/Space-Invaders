            [Instructions for Space Invaders]
This game has five Keys that is use to control the player and the game.
1. a
2. d
3. Space Bar                       
4. q
5. h

1> FOR CHANGING THE POSITIONS OF SPACE SHIP
"a" is for moving left
"d" is for moving right
2> FOR FIRERING THE BULLET
 press space bar
3> FOR QUITING THE GAME
 press "q"
4> FOR HELP
 press "h"
